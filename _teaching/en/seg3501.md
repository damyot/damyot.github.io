Undergraduate course on requirements engineering, taught (in French as
*Ingénierie des exigences*) in most Fall terms. The course covers requirements
elicitation, analysis, specification, and validation, including scenario- and
goal-oriented modelling with the [User Requirements Notation](http://www.usecasemaps.org/urn)
(URN).

Course materials are distributed through the University of Ottawa's learning
management system (Brightspace). Please contact me for the current syllabus.
