I regularly offer Capstone (SEG 4910/4911), Co-op, and Master's projects for
students interested in software engineering, requirements engineering, and
modelling. Topics vary from year to year and typically build on my active
research areas:

- Tool support for the [User Requirements Notation](http://www.usecasemaps.org/urn)
  (URN) and [jUCMNav](http://softwareengineering.ca/jucmnav/)
- Business process mining, modelling, and automation
- Requirements engineering for healthcare, well-being, and aging
- Legal and regulatory compliance, and smart contracts (Symboleo)

If you would like to discuss a project, please email me with your interests and
background. Current students should also consult the [students](/students/) page.
