I joined the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the
University of Ottawa in February 2002, after a few years as Senior Researcher at
[Mitel Networks](https://www.mitel.com/). From 2002 to 2013, I was Associate
Rapporteur on Formal Languages and Telecommunication Software at the
International Telecommunication Union (ITU-T), where I led the development of the
[User Requirements Notation](http://www.usecasemaps.org/urn) (URN) standard. I
also lead the development of the [jUCMNav tool](http://softwareengineering.ca/jucmnav/)
for URN modelling and analysis.

My research interests span many domains:

- Software Engineering
- Scenario- and Goal-oriented Requirements Engineering
- User Requirements Notation (URN)
- Business Process Mining and Modelling
- Healthcare Informatics
- Legal and Regulatory Compliance
- Smart Contracts

I hold a Ph.D. and an M.Sc. from the University of Ottawa (2001 and 1994), and a
B.Sc. from Laval University (1992), all in Computer Science. I am a member of the
OIQ (professional engineer) and a Senior Member of the IEEE. I serve on the
editorial boards of [Requirements Engineering (REJ)](https://www.springer.com/journal/766)
and [Software and Systems Modeling (SoSyM)](https://www.sosym.org/), both Springer.
I am a member of the Steering Committee of the IEEE International
[Requirements Engineering](http://requirements-engineering.org/) Conference and a
former Chair of the [SDL Forum Society](http://sdl-forum.org/).

I am also affiliated with the
[Contract Specification and Monitoring Lab](https://sites.google.com/uottawa.ca/csmlab),
the [LIFE Research Institute](https://www.uottawa.ca/life/en),
the [IBM/Telfer Centre for Business Analytics and Performance](http://sites.telfer.uottawa.ca/cbap/),
the [Institut du savoir Montfort (ISM)](https://www.savoirmontfort.ca/en/), and
the [Telfer Health Transformation Exchange (THTex)](http://sites.telfer.uottawa.ca/thtex/).
