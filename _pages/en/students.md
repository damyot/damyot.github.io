I supervise graduate students (M.Sc., M.C.S., and Ph.D.) and undergraduate
research projects at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.
My students work across my main research areas:

- Scenario- and goal-oriented requirements engineering, and the
  [User Requirements Notation](http://www.usecasemaps.org/urn) (URN)
- Business process modelling, mining, and automation
- Healthcare informatics and requirements engineering for well-being and aging
- Legal and regulatory compliance, and smart contracts (Symboleo)

**Prospective students:** I welcome motivated students with a strong background
in software engineering or computer science. Please email me with your CV, a
short statement of research interests, and your transcripts. See my
[publications](/publications/) for examples of recent student work, and the list
of [potential projects](/projects/).
